# Cover Letter Structure (starting point, not a script)

**[Date]**

Dear [Hiring Manager name, or "the {{Company}} team" if unknown],

**Opening (1-2 sentences):** Name the role, the company, and one concrete, specific reason you're drawn to it — never generic ("I am excited about this opportunity").

**Middle (1-2 short paragraphs):** Connect 1-2 real achievements from `identity/achievements.md` directly to the posting's stated requirements. Be specific with numbers/outcomes where they exist.

**Why this company (1 short paragraph):** Reference something concrete from company research — a product, a mission statement, recent news — not boilerplate flattery.

**Closing (1-2 sentences):** Reiterate interest, note availability, thank them.

[Sign-off matching identity/writing-style.md]
[Name]
