# Common Screening Questions

<!-- Reference list used as a starting point by apply-agent during live /nemohire:apply answering,

- Tell me about yourself.
- Why do you want to work here?
- Why this role?
- What are your strengths?
- What's an area you're working to improve?
- Describe a time you led a project or team.
- Describe a conflict you navigated at work.
- What's an achievement you're most proud of?
- What are your salary expectations?
- What's your availability / notice period?
- Are you authorized to work in [country]? Do you require sponsorship?
