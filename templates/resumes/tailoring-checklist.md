# Resume Tailoring Checklist

- [ ] Reordered experience/skills to surface what's most relevant to this posting first
- [ ] Mirrored key terminology from the posting where it genuinely matches real experience
- [ ] No fabricated skills, titles, dates, or achievements
- [ ] Quantified achievements preserved (numbers, outcomes) — not diluted into vague language
- [ ] Length appropriate for the role's seniority (typically 1-2 pages)
- [ ] Contact info and links match `identity/profile.md`
- [ ] Any requirement the candidate doesn't meet is noted as a gap, not silently faked
